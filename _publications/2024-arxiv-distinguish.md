We created an educational guide to help people identify AI-generated imagery by recognizing telltale signs across five artifact categories: anatomical, stylistic, functional, physics violations, and sociocultural inconsistencies. We compiled a dataset of 138 AI-created images, 9 social media images, and 42 authentic photographs to demonstrate these patterns.
