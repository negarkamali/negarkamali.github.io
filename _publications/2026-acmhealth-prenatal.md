We explore patient expectations and preferences for machine learning-driven just-in-time adaptive interventions (JITAIs) in prenatal stress management. Through co-design sessions with pregnant individuals, we identify key factors that influence acceptance and effectiveness of AI-powered health interventions, emphasizing the importance of explanation and patient autonomy in ML-driven healthcare tools.
